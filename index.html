FAQ

Common questions

Is this for the new IB Biology syllabus?

Yes. The resources are written to be syllabus-agnostic — the IA criteria and marking standards have not changed between the old and new syllabus. Everything in BioMate applies to both.

Is this suitable for both SL and HL students?

Yes. The Internal Assessment requirements and marking criteria are identical for SL and HL Biology. BioMate applies equally to both levels.

What format do I receive the resources in?

All resources are delivered as secure PDF files immediately after purchase. The Planning Template is also available in an editable format so students can type directly into it.

Can my teacher see what I've written?

No. The PDF resources are for personal use only. If your school uses the BioMate IA Workspace (currently in pilot), your teacher can monitor your progress — but the standalone PDF resources are private.

Does BioMate write the IA for me?

No. BioMate provides structured guidance, worked examples, and frameworks. All scientific investigation, data collection, analysis, and writing must be the student's own work. Using BioMate does not violate IB academic integrity policies.

Are classroom licences available?

Yes. If you are a teacher purchasing for a class, classroom licences covering 10, 20, or 30 students are available at a reduced per-student rate. Contact us at biomate.hello@gmail.com for details.
